3. Blackberry devices support the following accessibility features for people with disabilities or impairments:
> speakerphone
> visual, audible, and vibration alerts and notifications
> hearing aid compatibility (on some models)
> assignable ring tones to identify callers
> customizable fonts and themes
> browser zoom
> reverse contrast and grayscale display settings
> autoText
> SureType® technology with predictive text
> SurePress™ touch screen

Windows Phone 7 has these accessibility options:

> Adjust theme and visual scheme to make it easier to see
> Options to use phone with a hearing aid
> Allows for speech commands to make calls, search the web, open apps, and listen to text messages
